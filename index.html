About Us

Welcome to Destiny Safaris Kenya

Welcome to Destiny Safaris, your trusted local tour operator specializing in East African adventures. Based in the heart of Nairobi, Kenya, we pride ourselves on offering unforgettable experiences across the region, backed by our extensive knowledge of the diverse tourism offerings in East Africa.

With over a decade of experience, we are passionate about showcasing the best of East Africa, from its breathtaking wildlife to its stunning landscapes. Whether you're seeking a thrilling wildlife safari, an invigorating mountain climbing expedition, a relaxing beach holiday, or a specially tailored family or student package, we have the perfect itinerary for you.

Our Services

WorldWide Tours

While we specialize in East African travel, Destiny Safaris offers customized worldwide tours for those seeking global adventures. Leveraging our extensive network of international partners, we can craft personalized itineraries to popular destinations across Europe, Asia, the Americas, and beyond. Whether you’re interested in exploring ancient cities, discovering new cultures, or enjoying scenic landscapes, we provide top-tier service and tailored travel experiences across the globe. From flight bookings to accommodations and guided tours, our worldwide offerings ensure a seamless journey wherever your wanderlust takes you.

Hotel Reservation

At Destiny Safaris, we take the stress out of hotel reservations by securing accommodations that match your preferences and budget. Whether you're looking for luxurious first-class hotels, eco-friendly safari lodges, or charming boutique accommodations, our long-standing relationships with top hotels across East Africa allow us to negotiate the best rates for our clients. We ensure that your stay is comfortable and convenient, with properties offering prime locations, exceptional service, and top-notch amenities. From bustling cities to remote wilderness, we guarantee the perfect place to relax after each day's adventure.

Travel Guides

Our team of professional travel guides at Destiny Safaris are experts in East Africa, offering deep insights into the region's culture, wildlife, and history. They enhance your journey by providing personalized attention, guiding you through the stunning landscapes of Kenya, Tanzania, and beyond. With years of experience, they ensure your safety and comfort while sharing fascinating details about the destinations you visit, from the savannahs of the Serengeti to the shores of the Indian Ocean. With our knowledgeable guides, you'll experience the true essence of Africa, leaving with lasting memories and a deeper appreciation of the region.

Event Management

Destiny Safaris specializes in seamless event management for both corporate and private groups. Whether you're organizing a business retreat, incentive trip, or a fundraising event, our experienced team is dedicated to making your event a success. From crafting personalized itineraries to managing logistics such as venue selection, accommodation, and activities, we handle every detail to ensure a memorable experience. With our in-depth knowledge of East Africa and partnerships with premier venues, we can tailor events to suit your specific goals, budget, and group size, leaving you free to focus on the bigger picture.
